Ezequiel Braga is a MSc student in Applied Mathematics and Data Science at FGV EMAp. He earned his undergraduate degree in Applied Mathematics at FGV EMAp, where he was a scholarship holder of the Center for the Development of Mathematics and Sciences (CDMC). Originally from Arapiraca, Alagoas, he completed all of his elementary and high school education in public schools, where he was a three-time medalist in the Brazilian Public School Mathematics Olympiad (OBMEP). During his academic life, he served as a teaching assistant five times and was a scholarship holder in both the Scientific and Master's Initiation Program (PICME) and the Institutional Program for Scientific Initiation Scholarships (PIBIC), where he had the opportunity to begin engaging with graduate-level work and developed an interest in research and problem-solving. His work focuses on Bayesian modeling, with a particular interest in power priors. He serves as a research assistant on the hdbayes R package project and Mosqlimate project. His primary research interests lie in Biostatistics, especially in Bayesian and Computational Statistics.
